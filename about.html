About Richfield Connect

Our Mission

To digitally transform student engagement through a secure, institution-focused social platform that fosters academic excellence and professional collaboration.

Key Features

  • Personal academic profiles
  • Real-time interactive feed

  • Professional networking within Richfield

Privacy First

Unlike commercial platforms, Richfield Connect is designed with institutional standards and data privacy expectations at its core.
